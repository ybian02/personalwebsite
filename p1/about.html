Hi! This is me.
Looking really happy :)

I am a maker, an artist, an interaction designer.

I love the process of translating product strategy and consumer insights into digital experiences for all. For the past three years, I have been commuting between Tufts and SMFA to pursue my degrees in International Relations and Studio Art. I have also spent a semester abroad studying Interaction Design at Central Saint Martins, London. When I am not designing by myself, I work on projects at Bean+Co, a creative studio run by four friends.

I'm interested in a lot of things, but especially travel, fitness and sustainability. I have lived in three different continents and five cities, and the list continues to grow. These days, I'm emjoying my last few months in college and getting excited about where life takes me next.
