Branding the first hackathon that treats designers equally important as programmers -

Polyhack

Creative Direction | Event Design | Branding

Medium: Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch

Polyhack is a hackathon that welcomes students from all disciplines, especially designers. As one of the designer mentor and producer of the hackathon, I was in charge of creating visual identity and producing marketing material. The logo is made of overlaing triangles, reflecting the interdisciplinary nature of the hachathon. The logo animation of triangles "coming together" strengthens this theme, too.

This project is created in collaboration with Shanshan Duan, Bruno Olmedo, and Emily Lin.
