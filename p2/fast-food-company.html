Challenging our view on fast fashion products from the way clothing is staged

Forever Fast Food Company

CREATIVE DIRECTION | INSTALLATION

Medium: Adobe Creative Suite, inket on paper, vinyl cut, found objects

This project is a critique on consumer behaviour regarding fast fashion products. The clothes disguised as fast food objects are originally found in a discard bin. They are staged as value packs from a fast food brand, Forever Fast Food. The retro look of the menu makes viewers to rethink our definition of "old" and "new" because all the discarded clothes were still perfectly wearable. This project was featured in CSM Graphic Design Stage 2 Show.
