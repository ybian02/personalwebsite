Tufts Daily App

UI/UX | Mobile Design

Medium: Sketch, Adobe XD

I worked with the Tufts Daily to design a mobile app interface for the school's biggest news publication. The app will be used by Tufts students to stay updated with news around campus and in greater Boston area. The publication already has a brand guideline and a functional website, and the editors are hoping that the app mobile app will attract more readers for the Daily and that it will provide a framework to re-redesign the existing website.
