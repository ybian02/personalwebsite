Adopt a Kiosk

Creative Direction | Illustration | Website Design

Medium: Skilkscreening on paper, html, CSS, jQuery

For my first brief “Capture London” at Central Saint Martins during my semester abroad, I was given 4 weeks to produce 2 publications revolving a common theme. The theme I focused on is “Adopt a Kiosk”, which looks at the future of British iconic red phone boxes. It is inevitable that old-dated structures and systems will be abandoned as technology develops, but through design we can give new lives to those structures that no longer fit our needs.
