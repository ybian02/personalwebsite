Documenting my commute journey in illustrations -

Subway People

Illustrations | Print | Social Media

Medium: Marker on paper, water color on paper

My drawing series “Subway People” started off with a very simple motivation: to kill time on subway rides. When other people were staring at their phones on subway, I decided to capture them in pen — for me people are much more interesting than electronic devices. During the summer of 2014, I consistently posted my illustrations on social media and eventually sold over 50 copies of my illustrated sketchbook online.
