ManUp Interface Design

UI/UX | Website Design

Medium: Sketch, Adobe XD, Illustrator

I am currently working as a UI/UX Designer for a healthcare product that aims to empower guys to achieve their best in life through improved health. The 16-week long program requires users to complete 6 different weekly tasks alongside 2 other optional actions. My challenge was to create user flow and interface designs that ensure users will complete the tasks without being too demanding.
