Bean + Co

Art Direction | Website Design

A project with Annie Lye, Kristie Le and Jade Chan

Bean and Co is an online agency covering the latest projects in photography, design and tech. As one of the founding members, I created the branding guideline for Bean + Co and designed the website for the agency. Aligned with our mission to make brilliant ideas soar and collaborate with clients, the Bean + Co brand is passionate, friendly and bold. The entire branding, design and web development process was collaborative and took about 6 months. Take a look at our live website or learn about our story.
